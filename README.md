# BharatEV Franchise Operations & ROI Intelligence Dashboard

An AI-assisted operational intelligence platform designed for EV franchise management, executive monitoring, operational workflows, and AI-assisted decision-making.

This project was built with a focus on:
- executive-level operational visibility
- realistic startup execution workflows
- AI-assisted operational intelligence
- franchise expansion monitoring
- operational bottleneck tracking
- role-based dashboard personalization

# Key Features

## Role-Based Executive Dashboards
Dynamic dashboards designed for:
- CEO
- CTO
- Co-Founder
- Business Heads
- Operations Teams
- Regional Managers

Each role receives:
- customized KPIs
- personalized AI insights
- operational alerts
- workflow visibility
- business intelligence summaries

## AI-Assisted Operational Intelligence
Includes:
- AI fallback monitoring
- AI confidence visibility
- operational recommendations
- staffing alerts
- onboarding bottlenecks
- escalation tracking
- AI vs Human workflow visibility

# Design Philosophy

The dashboard intentionally avoids:
- generic admin panel styling
- overly “perfect” business metrics
- marketing website aesthetics

Instead, the focus was on:
- operational realism
- startup execution complexity
- enterprise usability
- AI-assisted workflows
- executive readability
- EV-focused operational intelligence
